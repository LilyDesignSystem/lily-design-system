share-chooser — strategies

The same targets under all three strategies. On a device with a native share sheet (most phones, Safari, Edge) the first two open the OS sheet and never show the list; the third always shows the list.

strategy="auto" (default)

Native sheet when the browser has one, list otherwise.

strategy="native"

Always attempts the sheet. With no sheet available, nothing happens.

strategy="list"

Never uses the sheet. The only way to guarantee your destinations are reachable.
